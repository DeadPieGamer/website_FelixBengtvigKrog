Retrace

Premise

Retrace is a first person puzzle game that centers around 'the Transplacer', a gun that shoots bullets that move towards you from wherever you're pointing. With this gun, you're tasked to solve a variety of puzzles, hitting different switches with the bullets.

It won the game jam "Hiddel's NES Jam", for which the game was made within 72 hours with the theme of "Reverse".
